Welcome to Random Tour 3000

This app lets you explore Berlin's public transport stations at random. The main areas of the app are explained below.

Home – Roll a station

Press the ROLL button or shake your phone to draw a random station. A 20 second cooldown avoids accidental double rolls. Disable it under settings if you prefer instant rolls.

Station view

After rolling you see details about the station: served lines and your visit history. Mark the station as visited, add notes or attach photos. Visits can be edited or removed later.

Visited

The Visited Stations tab lists every place you have checked off so far. Sort the list by name or by date to review your progress.

All stations

The All Stations view shows the complete network. Use the search to find places manually and add visits that were not rolled.

Backups

Your data is stored only in the browser. Use Settings → Backups to export a JSON file or import a previous backup so nothing gets lost.

Settings & account

Change the language, define a home station for travel time calculations, toggle the roll cooldown and manage your account (change password, log out or delete account).

Enjoy your random tour through Berlin!
